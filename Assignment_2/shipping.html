Everything you need to know about how we get our honey to your door, and what happens if things don't go as planned.

Shipping Policy

We ship all orders from our farm in California using carefully selected carriers. All jars are bubble-wrapped and packaged in sturdy cardboard boxes to ensure your honey arrives safely and on time.

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking details as soon as your package is on its way.

Return Policy

Your satisfaction is our highest priority. If you are not completely happy with your purchase, we will make it right.

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

Returns on unopened products are accepted within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be in their original, sealed condition.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 7 days at support@goldenhive.com with a photo, and we will promptly send a replacement or issue a full refund.

How to Initiate a Return

Email our support team with your order number and reason for return. We'll provide a prepaid return label and process your refund within 5–7 business days of receiving the item.

Still Have Questions?

Our friendly support team is available Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm PST. We're happy to help with any shipping concerns or return requests.
