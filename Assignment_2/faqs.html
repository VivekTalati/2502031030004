Products & Ingredients

Yes, all Golden Hive honey is 100% pure, raw, and unfiltered. We never pasteurize or add any artificial syrups or preservatives to our products. It comes straight from the hive to the jar.
Crystallization is a natural process that proves your honey is raw and unfiltered. It does not affect the quality or taste. To return it to a liquid state, simply place the jar in a bowl of warm (not boiling) water and stir gently.
Pure honey never expires! It is known to last indefinitely when stored properly in a sealed container away from direct sunlight and extreme moisture.

Shipping & Returns

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping within the US takes 3-5 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.
If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we accept returns on unopened jars within 30 days of delivery. For damaged items, please contact our support team immediately with a photo for a replacement.
Currently, we only ship within the United States and Canada. We are working on expanding our shipping options in the near future.
